## 📌 Overview

**SHERNI** is India's free women's safety guide — built for every Indian girl and woman. It delivers practical, verified, and actionable safety knowledge in a single web application.

No login. No subscription. No ads. No data collection. **Free forever.**



## 🎯 Mission

> *"Safety knowledge should not be a privilege — it should be a birthright."*

SHERNI was built for:
- The girl walking home alone at 9PM
- The college student being groomed online
- The young woman who doesn't know she can file a Zero FIR from any police station in India
- Every sister who deserves to know her rights, her power, and her options
