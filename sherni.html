🌟 Most Important Skill — Awareness Saves Lives

Be Aware · Observe · Act

The most powerful safety skill isn't a move — it's your eyes. Observing properly lets you avoid danger before it reaches you.

👁️
Look Around Carefully
Wherever you are — school gate, bus stop, street — scan for people, vehicles, and anything unusual.
🚪
Memorise Exits Always
Always note: where is the nearest door, shop, crowd, or open space? Exits first — always.
🗺️
Know Your Route
Memorise safe spots on your daily path — shops, police booths, school gates, busy street corners.
🦋
Trust Your Instincts
If something feels off, it probably is. Your gut is your best safety system — always listen to it.

🏪Exit at the next safe stop — move to crowded area immediately

▶

🏪Move to shops, main roads, or public offices — never isolated areas
🗺️Keep escape routes in mind — always know the nearest safe spot
💥If cornered: use bag, pen, umbrella, or spray — aim for eyes, nose, groin — then RUN
Example: Stranger offers ride — refuse firmly, cross street to café, call friend, activate SOS app on phone.
6️⃣

▶

🏃‍♀️Escape immediately — do not negotiate or reason
👮Alert security or call 112 as soon as you're safe
